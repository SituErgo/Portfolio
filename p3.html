Ergou


Instruction
Click left mouse button to change the shape and the color of the monster.



Design process

STEP 1 Idea
Every time people click the left mouse, the program will generate a new tiny monster with a different shape, color, and eyes.

STEP 2 Parameter
SHAPE: the control point of the shape
COLOR: the RGB value
EYES: the position of the center point of the eyes； the circle radius(the eyeball)； the ellipse width, and the height(whites of the eyes)

STEP 3 User interaction
To make it more interesting, I made the eyes follow the cursor and the monster move with the cursor.
